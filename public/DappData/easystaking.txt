-= Service Name: Easy Staking =-
-= Service Type: DApp or Service =-

-= This information was updated and correct as of: 06/09/2020 =-

-= Note: This section accesses my Wallet and guides on the removal of Assets
Please follow the instructions carefully =-

Easy Staking is a DApp that is allows users to deposit an Ethereum based Asset to in
order to assist with providing a service in return the user earns more of that Ethereum
based Asset. The Ethereum Asset being used in this DApp / service is known as STAKE.

The aim of this section is to remove the Assets I have in Easy Staking and return them
to the Wallet and Dapp Gateway.

Within this section of the document we will cover the following topics.

-= Connect to Easy Staking =-
-= Remove STAKE from Easy Staking =-

These may be referenced in other sections from other services in this document.
These topics may not be conducted in order and may need to be conducted multiple times
throughout this document.

-= Connect to Easy Staking =-

In your internet browser search/ address bar type: "easy-staking.xdaichain.com".

Press Enter to access the website.

Click "Connect" wallet in the centre of the page.

Once clicked, click on the Wallet and DApp Gateway being used.

A new screen will pop-up asking for permission for the website to interact with the
Wallet and DApp Gateway.

Select account with my Ethereum Address.

Click Connect.

If successful, the page will reload with details of how much STAKE I have staked with
Easy Staking.

-= Remove STAKE from Easy Staking =-

Click on the "Easy Staking" text in the top left of the webpage.

There will be table at the bottom of the page that has all of the stakes I currently
have on Easy Staking.

For each of the stakes you will need to follow the same process in order to remove
the STAKE Ethereum based Assets from Easy Staking.

Click on the "Details" text on one of the stakes.

Navigate to the Instant Withdrawal element on the right hand side of the page.
This will explain that you are requesting instant access to the staked assets rather
than waiting for 12 hours with the Scheduled withdrawal. This does come at a cost with
the percentage fee shown currently at 2%.

Click the "2%" button.

Click the "Max" text. In the Instant Withdrawal element on the right of the page.

Click the "Withdraw" button.

You need to Confirm the transaction with the Wallet and DApp Gateway. As the Wallet and
DApp Gateway was connected previously another pop-up of the Wallet and DApp Gateway
will appear on the screen.

Click Confirm on the new Wallet and DApp Gateway pop-up.

The Transaction will be submitted to withdraw the Asset and it now needs to be
approved by the Ethereum Blockchain. Approval can take seconds, minutes, hours or days
depending on how many Transactions globally are awaiting approval.

The  Wallet and Dapp Gateway will give confirmation when the Transaction has
been approved.

If the Transaction is rejected try the above steps again.

Once approved the STAKE Ethereum based Asset will now have been successfully
transferred the connected Wallet and DApp Gateway.

Repeat the above steps for each of the stakes in the table found when clicking on
the "Easy Staking" text in the top left of the webpage.

End of Easy Staking section.
